About Us

RCC camping company was started in 1990 by Euroka Camping, Eliza Camping, and Kevin Camping.Our goal was to deliver a type of customer service that was nonexistent in the industry.

With nine current offices and several others in the works, Camping Companies is one of the largest wholly owned repossession companies in the nation. Our strong commitment to our employees and clients since the company was started has contributed to our continued growth. Currently almost twenty percent of Camping Companies employees have been with the company for ten years or more, and numerous others have been with the company well over five years. A fact that makes the companies owners very proud.

We offer various kinds of camping equipment,such as tent, bag,furniture,etc.,and equipment packages and camping car booking.

Our mission is to use our experience to serve our clients needs in a fast professional manner,to continue to grow and provide better opportunities for our employees and a wider service area for our clients.
